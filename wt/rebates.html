Income Tax Rebate

Rebates

Income tax benefits for senior citizens were further improved in the year 2020. They were granted an exemption of up to Rs. 3 lakh. Additionally, under section 80D, there was a provision for a maximum deduction of Rs. 25,000 concerning medical insurance premium.

In the Finance Bill of 2020, adjustments were made to enhance investment options. The investment ceiling in a Pension Plan was raised from Rs. 10,000 to Rs. 1.5 lakh. Moreover, investments in a five-year fixed deposit with a scheduled bank were made eligible for tax deduction, falling within the overall limit of Rs. 1.5 lakh. However, it's important to note that the collective limit for exemption under both section 80C and 80CCC (Pension) remained capped at Rs. 1.5 lakh.Income tax benefits for senior citizens were further improved in the year 2020. Eligibility

Senior Citizens of 65 years and above during the relevant previous year.

How To Avail?

Contact the Income Tax Department and fill in the form 2(c).
