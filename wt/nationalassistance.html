National Social Assistance Programme

The National Old Age Pension Scheme provides Central Assistance to eligible individuals based on the following criteria:

  • Age Requirement: The applicant, whether male or female, should be 65 years or older.

  • Destitution Criterion: The applicant must be classified as destitute, signifying a lack of regular means of subsistence derived from personal income or financial support from family members or other sources.

The Central Government allocates a monthly old age pension amount of Rs. 200 to eligible beneficiaries under this scheme. Implementation of the scheme takes place in the various States and Union Territories through Panchayats and Municipalities. Additionally, the State's contribution may vary from one State to another.
